## Structured Feedback Questions (send after they've tried it)

**Subject:** Quick 5 questions about your Takeoff.ai experience

Thanks for testing Takeoff.ai! I'd love to hear your honest feedback:

1. **Accuracy** - How close was the estimate to what you'd expect? What was off?
2. **Missing rooms** - Did it miss any rooms or detect rooms that don't exist?
3. **Pricing** - Were the material/labor costs in the right ballpark for your area?
4. **Would you use this?** - If it were more accurate, would you use it for preliminary estimates?
5. **What's missing?** - What feature or improvement would make this tool useful for your daily workflow?

No need to write a novel - even a one-liner for each is super helpful.

Thanks,
[Your name]
